About Us

At EcoMeal, we believe that great meals start with what you already have. Our mission is to help you reduce food waste and maximize flavor by providing smart, ingredient-based meal suggestions. Whether you're working with leftovers, pantry staples, or fresh produce, EcoMeal turns what’s available into something delicious. Join us in making food more sustainable—one meal at a time!
